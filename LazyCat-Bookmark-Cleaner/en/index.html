Frequently Asked Questions

How to clean broken bookmark links?

After installing LazyCat Bookmark Cleaner, click its icon in the toolbar. The system will automatically scan your bookmarks and identify broken links. You can select which bookmarks to clean, confirm your selection, and click delete to complete the cleanup.

How to clean duplicate bookmarks?

After installing LazyCat Bookmark Cleaner, click its icon in the toolbar. The system will automatically scan your bookmarks and identify duplicates. You can select which bookmarks to keep, confirm your selection, and click delete to complete the cleanup.

How to view bookmark statistics?

After installing LazyCat Bookmark Cleaner, click its icon in the toolbar. Click the 'Bookmark Analysis' button on the page to view your bookmark statistics.

Which browsers are supported?

Currently supports Chrome and Edge browsers.
